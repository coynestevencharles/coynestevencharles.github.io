Google Scholar Profile

I am studying Natural Language Processing, particularly the intersection of NLP and education. My current research projects focus on generating constructive feedback comments on English learners' writing and on automated assessment of English learner speech. Other subfields of interest include intelligent tutoring systems, automated essay evaluation, and interactive tools for writing assistance, knowledge management, or human-AI co-creation.
